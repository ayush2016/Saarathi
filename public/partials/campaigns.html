Campaigns

Visit to rescued victims at an Orphanage


Our vision began with the visit to rescued victims at Snehalaya, Guwahati. This was the first step which gained us an acquaintance with the real state both mental and emotional of the underprivileged children. An event which constituted of interaction with children victimised of different incidents through art and cultural activities, helped us discover a deeper insight of them.

Documentary Screening at IIT Guwahati


With an ambition of learning causes, impact and other crucial factors involved in the web of human trafficking at a more detailed level documentary screening of a movie on Human Trafficking was organised. It was coupled with an interactive talk by Prof. Sawmya Ray with the Saarathi team and other interested individuals.

Oriental session at IIT Guwahati


An oriental session for Saarathi as NGO organised to present the intricacy of human trafficking web and its present scenario in northeast region to the individuals at IIT Guwahati with an aim to awaken more people to raise voice and contribute towards its prevention.
