Human trafficking and the role of the government in Assam

Nayani Sharma | Cotton College State University

Every year, thousands of men, women and children fall into the hands of traffickers, in their own countries and abroad. Almost every country in the world is affected by trafficking, whether as a country of origin, transit or destination for victims. Human Trafficking is a form of modern slavery where people profit from the control and exploitation of others. Exploitation includes, at a minimum, the exploitation of the prostitution of others or other forms of sexual exploitation, forced labour or services, slavery or practices similar to slavery, servitude or the removal of organs.

If you pray for a better world, then start from yourself!

Sagar Garg | IIT Guwahati

You earn well, dine and live well. You sleep soundly, dreaming of a better tomorrow, for yourself and hopefully for others too. You know it's a corrupt living, each day a race to score your dominion over another. You can see some are left way down on the ladder, but you possibly can't imagine their world. You got your own race to run. When you scroll through the violence of the world streaming live on your phone, sometimes you let out a silent prayer as well for those unfortunate. 'Someone should help them!', you murmur to know one. 'But what can I do?' And at this juncture, you pause but never stop. You know you mean well but are just helpless.
