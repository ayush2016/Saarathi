Human trafficking and the role of the government in Assam

Nayani Sharma | Cotton College State University

Every year, thousands of men, women and children fall into the hands of traffickers, in their own countries and abroad. Almost every country in the world is affected by trafficking, whether as a country of origin, transit or destination for victims. Human Trafficking is a form of modern slavery where people profit from the control and exploitation of others.
