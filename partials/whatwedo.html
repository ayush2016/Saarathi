What We Do

SAARATHI aims to stop the heinous crime of Human Trafficking in India, specifically in Assam region in all its forms and restore the lives of the survivors. Human Trafficking is still a taboo in our society and hence we wish to spread awareness among audiences in various regions through our campaigns and educate them about the issue. We seek help and participation from volunteers and law enforcement authorities to inform us about these practices and safeguard the victims.
We have adopted Prevention Principle. Our prime objective is to create awareness about this vicious cycle and to raise voice against it. We are looking forward to coming up with a sustainable model of creating awareness with the help of technological intervention.


Legal Advice and Assistance

We provide legal advice and assistance to the deprived, activists, organisational representatives and others affected and/or working on issues related to human trafficking.



Raise Awareness


Saarathi works dedicatedly towards enhancing the collective consciousness of the society towards human trafficking. Our volunteers organise campaigns, street plays, seminars and workshops.



Research


Saarathi engages religiously in conducting in-depth research on Human Trafficking. It also extends its research area to the socio-economic and political policy reforms undertaken by various governments around the world in order to fight against the trade of human flesh.



Community Development

We work closely with Anganwadi workers in order to facilitate the development of rural spaces and communities and bringing empowerment to the people affected.
